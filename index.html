About

Full-time travelers.

We have been traveling full time and enjoying every pocket of the world. We started in March of 2022 and haven't been home since.

We love seeing the best possible and the most local possible everywhere we go. We have visited all seven continents, and every wonder — Seven Wonders of the World, the ancient wonders, the natural wonders, the wonders of nature.

We have drank at every World's 50 Best Bar from 2009 to present. We have had over three hundred and fifty Michelin-starred meals, and have paired every one with street food before or after.

We have stayed at almost every Aman, Belmond, and Four Seasons, and have camped in −30° yurts, in cars, on cots, and on the floor. We have been scuba diving in over forty countries, and bungee jumping, hang gliding, skydiving — everything in between.

We love to do and try it all. Cheers.
