About us

Two carry-ons, no plan.

We have been traveling full time and enjoying every pocket of the world.

Started traveling March of 2022 and haven't been home since. We love seeing the best possible and the most local possible everywhere we go.

We have visited all seven continents — the wonders of the world, the ancient wonders, the natural wonders, and the wonders of nature. We've drunk at every World's 50 Best Bar from 2009 to present. We've had over three hundred and fifty Michelin-starred meals, and paired every one with street food before or after. We've stayed at almost every Aman, Belmond, and Four Seasons, and have camped in −30° yurts, cars, cots, and on the floor.

We have been scuba diving in over forty countries, as well as bungee jumping, hang gliding, skydiving, and everything in between. Love to do and try it all.

Cheers,
Max & Hailey
